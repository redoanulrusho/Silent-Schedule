# Silent-Schedule

**Focus on your prayers, not your phone.**

[![Download APK](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-App_Release_v1.0.0-green.svg?style=for-the-badge&logo=android)](https://github.com/redoanulrusho/Silent-Schedule/releases/download/v1.0.0/app-release.apk)

Silent-Schedule is a smart, easy-to-use Android utility application designed to help you maintain perfect concentration during your daily prayers (Namaj). We all know the worry of accidentally leaving our phone ringer on inside the Masjid. This app completely removes that hassle by automatically managing your phone's volume for you.

## Key Features

* **Automatic Silencing:** Automatically switches your mobile phone to silent mode during your scheduled Namaj times.
* **Distraction-Free Ibadah:** Ensures a peaceful and respectful environment in the mosque, allowing you and those around you to pray without sudden interruptions.
* **Set It and Forget It:** Simply configure your daily prayer schedule once, and the app will quietly handle the rest in the background every day.
* **Seamless Volume Restoration:** Once the prayer duration is over, the app intelligently restores your phone to its previous ringer settings so you never miss an important call afterward.

## How to Install

1. Tap the **Download** badge above to get the `app-release.apk` file.
2. Open the downloaded file on your Android phone.
3. If prompted, allow "Install from unknown sources" in your device settings.
4. Open the app, set your Namaj schedule, and enjoy peace of mind!
